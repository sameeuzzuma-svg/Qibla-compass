About Qibla

The Qibla is the direction that Muslims face when performing their prayers (Salah). This direction points toward the Kaaba in the Masjid al-Haram mosque in Mecca, Saudi Arabia.

The Qibla direction is calculated based on your current location using spherical trigonometry. The compass uses your device's sensors to determine the correct direction relative to magnetic north.

Usage Tips
  • For best results, hold your device flat and rotate slowly until the red Qibla indicator aligns with the compass arrow
  • Make sure you've enabled location services on your device
  • In areas with magnetic interference, the compass may need calibration
  • For prayer purposes, always confirm Qibla direction with local mosque when possible
