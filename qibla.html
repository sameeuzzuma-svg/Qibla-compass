Qibla Direction Compass

This Qibla Direction Compass shows the current qibla direction using your device's compass. The app utilizes your geographical location and device sensors to find the accurate direction to the Kaaba in Mecca.

For best results:

  • Stay away from magnets, metals, and electronic devices
  • Calibrate your compass by moving the device in a figure-8 pattern
  • Hold your device flat and parallel to the ground
  • Ensure location services are enabled

Compass Access Required

This compass requires access to your device's orientation sensor to determine the Qibla direction accurately.

Please follow these steps:

  • Allow location and motion permissions when prompted
  • Ensure your device supports compass functionality
  • For best results, calibrate your device by moving it in a figure-8 pattern
  • Try refreshing the page if permissions were denied

Note: This feature works best on mobile devices with built-in magnetometers.
